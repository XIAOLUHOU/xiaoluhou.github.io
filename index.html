I am currently a research fellow in cybersecurity laboratory at Nanyang Technological University. I work on hardware security, mostly on fault attacks.

I got my PhD degree in May 2017 from Division of Mathematical Sciences, School of Physical and Mathematical Sciences, Nanyang Technological University. My PhD supervisor is Assoc. Prof. Frédérique Oggier. My thesis is available here (pdf).

Research Interests:

  • Hardware security and cryptograph
  • Algebraic Number Theory
  • Construction of modular lattices
