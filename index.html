I am currently a Research Scientist at Physical Analysis & Cryptography Engineering (PACE) Lab, Temasek Laboratories, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore. My research focus is hardware security of cryptography algorithms.

I got my PhD degree in May 2017 from Division of Mathematical Sciences, School of Physical and Mathematical Sciences, Nanyang Technological University. My PhD supervisor was Assoc. Prof. Frédérique Oggier. My thesis is available here.

Research Interests:

  • Hardware security and cryptography
  • Fault injection and side-channel attacks
  • Neural network security
